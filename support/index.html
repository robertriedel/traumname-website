Frequently Asked Questions

How do I connect with my partner?

Go to Settings and share your family code with your partner. Your partner can enter the code when they first open the app.

What is a match?

A match occurs when you and your partner independently like the same name. Matches appear automatically on the Matches page.

How does the taste test work?

The taste test shows you name pairs and learns from your preferences. Afterwards, names are intelligently sorted according to your taste.

Can I undo a liked name?

Yes! On the Matches page under "My Selection", you can remove individual names by tapping the trash icon.

Which languages are supported?

Traumname is available in English, Deutsch, Español, Français, Português, Italiano, Nederlands, Türkçe, and Polski.

Is the app free?

Yes, Traumname is 100% free. No hidden costs.

How can I delete my account?

Send us an email at support@traumname.com and we will delete your account and all associated data.
