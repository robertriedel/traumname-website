Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 28, 2026

Traumname ("we", "us", "our") operates the Traumname mobile app (the "Service"). This page informs you about our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data.

1. What Data Do We Collect?

We only collect data that is necessary for using the app:

  • Account information: An anonymous user ID is created upon registration. You may optionally provide a first name.
  • Usage data: Which names you like or dislike, your taste profile settings, and language settings.
  • Family connection: A unique family code to connect you with your partner.

We do not collect email addresses, phone numbers, location data, contacts, or photos.

2. How Do We Use Your Data?

  • To provide you with personalized name suggestions based on your taste
  • To calculate matches between you and your partner
  • To save your settings and preferences

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely with Supabase, a US-based service that meets GDPR requirements. Data transmission is encrypted (TLS/SSL).

4. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell your data. We do not share personal data with third parties, except:

  • Supabase: Database and authentication service
  • Apple: App Store and anonymous analytics data where applicable

5. Your Rights (GDPR)

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have the following rights:

  • Right of access: You can request information about your stored data.
  • Right to erasure: You can request the deletion of your account and all associated data.
  • Right to data portability: You can request your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Right to object: You can object to the processing of your data at any time.

To exercise your rights, contact us at support@traumname.com.

6. Data Security

We employ industry-standard security measures to protect your data. These include encrypted connections, Row Level Security (RLS) at the database level, and secure authentication.

7. Children

Our Service is not directed at children under 16 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be published on this page.

9. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact us at:
support@traumname.com
